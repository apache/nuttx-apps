USAGE:
    netsensor_stream [options] topic

ARGUMENTS:
    topic          The uORB topic to stream (including devno). Ex: sensor_baro0

OPTIONS:
    -h             Display this help message and quit.
    -a <devno>     Specify the IP address to stream data to. Default: 127.0.0.1
    -p <port>      Specify the UDP port to stream data to. Default: 5555
